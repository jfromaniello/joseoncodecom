I will post here a little description of the concepts that I want to cover in the uNhAddins.Wpf component.

  • AOP notifiable behavior for domain entities (INotifyPropertyChanged and INotifyCollectionChanged).
  • IEditableObject behavior.
  • N undo/redo multifield like this.
  • A suitable way to work with the NHibernate.Validator and the WPF Validations (IDataErrorInfo).
  • I will work in a suitable way to register the domain entities to the Windsor Container given the fact that I will use AOP in domain entities.

Please leave a comment here or in the mailing list with any feature that you will like to have in this package.
